What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management system that enables the person to work with the time that they have rather than against it. It breaks your working session into 25 minute chunks separated by 5 minute breaks (an interval known as a pomodoro). After four pomodoros, you take a longer break of 15 minutes.
